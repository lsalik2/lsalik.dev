A modern C++17 command-line application that analyzes your Steam library and recommends what to play next. It combines an interactive questionnaire with a weighted scoring algorithm that considers playtime, genres, difficulty, ratings, and more to deliver personalized game recommendations.

Still work in progress, but with the eventual goal of having it published directly on Steam for free, and made open-source on Github.
