SLKARDS is a trading card game powered by a Discord bot. Players collect digital cards called **kards**, then trade, battle, sell, and evolve them within a Discord server. The ultimate goal is to build up a collection, prestige series, climb competitive ladders, and create an entirely custom 25-kard series of your own.

The game features 414 unique kards spread across 17 series, a turn-based PvP battle system with type matchups and special abilities, a player-driven marketplace economy, guild-like alliances with cooperative raids and alliance-vs-alliance warfare, and a deep progression loop tied together by periodic inventory wipes that keep the economy fresh.

It is currently accessible for beta testing in my Discord server, which can be found in the `~/contact` page under the esports category.
